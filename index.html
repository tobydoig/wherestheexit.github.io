Overview

Your goal is simply to get to the center of the maze. Yeah, there might be locked gates, a thieving monkey, psychedelics, an alien invasion and even angry seals involved, but that's just part of the fun.

Highlights

  • Free to download and play - jump in and start rolling.
  • Full 3D maze - swipe, tilt, twist and pinch to steer the maze; gravity does the rest.
  • Themed levels - 50 levels across 6 themes, each with its own look and feel.
  • Progression - mazes get harder, with new mechanics and challenges.
  • Power-ups - collect power-ups that show you the way, for a limited time.
